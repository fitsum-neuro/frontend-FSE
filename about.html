About Delta Rental

Connecting communities through shared mobility.

Our Mission

Delta P2P Rental Service is a web-based platform designed to facilitate peer-to-peer car rentals within Ethiopia. It aims to connect individual car owners with renters, addressing issues like inefficient vehicle utilization, limited rental options, and trust concerns prevalent in the current market. The platform will provide features for user registration, car listing, advanced search, booking management, ratings/reviews, and administrative oversight. The system is intended to be user-friendly, secure, scalable, and tailored to the Ethiopian context, supporting Delta Rental's strategic shift towards a P2P model.

Our Vision

We envision a future where transportation is more accessible, sustainable, and community-driven. By empowering individuals to share their vehicles, we aim to reduce the number of idle cars, offer more affordable transport options, and foster a sense of trust and collaboration within local communities across Ethiopia.
